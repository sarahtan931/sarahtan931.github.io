InTouch

Caught in a Jam

Western University

Caught in a Jam is a fully functional RPG game using Unity and C#.

Caught in a Jam is a 2D game, where the player can encounter new enemies and missions while navigating through multiple worlds. This game also includes character customization, different types of combat, and player movement.

Transit Tracer

SheHacks

Transit Tracer is a product designed to provide LTC passengers with a live visual display of their bus along with the upcoming stops and estimated time of arrival.

We extracted data and compared it to live GPS locations to provide the visual display with live time information.
